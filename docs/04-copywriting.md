## FAQ Copy

**Q: What type of leather do you use?**
A: We use exclusively full-grain leather sourced from European tanneries. This is the highest grade of leather — unaltered, showing natural grain patterns that develop into a rich patina over time.

**Q: How should I care for my leather?**
A: Artyblanc leather requires minimal care. Keep away from prolonged water exposure, store in the provided dust bag when not in use, and condition occasionally with a quality leather balm. Our Leather Care Guide provides detailed instructions.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes. We ship to over 50 countries. Orders over $150 receive complimentary express shipping. Standard delivery: 3-7 business days. Express: 1-3 business days.

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: We offer 30-day returns on all unworn items in original condition. Returns are complimentary within the US and UK. International return shipping is at the customer's expense.

**Q: Do your bags fit a laptop?**
A: Yes. The Voyager Tote fits up to 14", the Metropolitan Brief fits up to 15", and the Weekender includes a padded device sleeve for tablets.

**Q: Is Artyblanc a sustainable brand?**
A: We prioritize longevity over disposability. Our pieces are designed to last decades — which is the most sustainable approach to consumption. We use vegetable-tanned leathers where possible and offer carbon-neutral shipping.
